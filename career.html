CAREER IN KFC

At KFC we focus on Man, whether he is a visitor or a member of the large KFC family. This is why we work hard and invest so much in maintaining and developing an amazing work environment for all our Team Members. In our restaurants we apply modern management methods, we are constantly adapting to changes, we encourage initiative, we reward excellent performance and we constantly create opportunities for development for our People!

YOU WANT TO WORK WITH US?

Our Values

Training

At KFC, structured training is the key to success. We invest in the continuous education of our people. We individually identify training needs, provide training in our restaurants and at the same time carry out training programs and seminars, thus enhancing the skills of our people, improving their effectiveness and enriching the knowledge required by each job.

Our Culture

The Colonel's values are alive and well inside our walls- in our food, our people, our service and our restaurants. When you join KFC, you join the Colonel's family, and we always aim to make the Colonel proud. Our house rules? Be your best self, make a difference and have fun.

Development

At KFC we are constantly creating growth opportunities for all employees. The evolution path is always available for those who want to build their career in catering! If you want to belong to a company that is constantly evolving, offering a unique service experience to each of its visitors, then send us your CV.
